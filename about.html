About the company

Truck Rertge is committed to providing reliable and high-quality truck sales and rental services. Our diverse fleet is designed to meet the needs of both businesses and individuals, offering durable and efficient vehicles for any task.

We prioritize customer satisfaction by delivering exceptional service and flexible solutions. Whether you're purchasing or renting, Truck Rertge ensures that you have access to the best vehicles at competitive prices, making us your trusted partner in trucking.

History of our company

Truck Rertge was founded in 2018 by Alexander Grant with the vision of providing reliable truck sales and rentals. Despite facing challenges, including the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic, the company grew stronger under the leadership of Alexander and Co-founder Ethan Clarke. By 2020, Truck Rertge was well-established and recognized for its high-quality vehicles and customer service.

Since then, the company has continued to expand its fleet and services, meeting the growing needs of businesses and individuals. Truck Rertge remains committed to offering reliable solutions and exceptional customer experiences, positioning itself as a trusted leader in the trucking industry.
